This button is to be used on a Mozilla Browser to add a tab to see your Workflow Tasks, you could see this new tab clicking in your bookmarks and then select EplSite.

Before push button, remember to modify the text: "http://eplsite/wfbackendhtml.epl?uid=your_uid" and set your server values.

You can see your userID in the footer bar when you login to the WorkFlow System.
